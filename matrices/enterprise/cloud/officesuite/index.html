Office Suite Matrix

Below are the tactics and techniques representing the MITRE ATT&CK® Office Suite platform. The techniques below are known to target cloud-based office application suites such as Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace. Office application suites are SaaS platforms that typically combine email, chat, document management, and automation functionality for use in a collaborative environment. The Matrix contains information for the Office Suite platform.
